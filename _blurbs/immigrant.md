## You have the right to be treated equally.

Federal law prohibits:

- citizenship status discrimination in hiring, firing, or recruitment or referral for a fee
- national origin discrimination in hiring, firing, or recruitment or referral for a fee
- unfair documentary practices during the employment eligibility verification, including [Form I-9](https://www.uscis.gov/i-9) and [E-Verify](https://www.uscis.gov/e-verify)
- retaliation or intimidation

You may be rehired, allowed to keep working, or get paid for time when you should have been allowed to work based on these acts.
